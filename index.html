FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of digital products do you sell?
▼
We sell a wide range of premium digital products including website templates, UI component libraries, SaaS starter kits, admin dashboards, portfolio themes, e-commerce templates, and mobile app UI kits. All products are production-ready and fully documented.
Do I get lifetime access after purchasing?
▼
Yes! All one-time purchases include lifetime access to the product and all future updates. We continuously improve our products based on customer feedback and evolving web standards.
How does the service booking process work?
▼
After submitting a service request, our team reviews your requirements within 1 business day. We'll send a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing. Once approved, we begin work immediately with regular progress updates through your dashboard.
What payment methods do you accept?
▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise orders. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
Can I use the products for commercial projects?
▼
Absolutely. All products come with a commercial use license. You can use them in client projects, SaaS products, or any commercial application. The Pro and Enterprise plans also include extended licensing for large-scale deployment.
Do you offer refunds?
▼
Due to the digital nature of our products, we don't offer refunds after download. However, if you experience any issues with a product not matching its description, please contact our support team and we'll work to resolve it promptly.
