FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes, you can switch between our monthly and annual plans at any time. If you switch from a higher plan to a lower one, you'll be charged for the difference until the end of your billing cycle.
What happens after my free trial?
After your 14-day trial, you'll be prompted to enter payment details to continue. We'll never charge you without warning. If you don't subscribe, your account will be downgraded to read-only access — your downloaded products remain yours forever.
Do I own the products I download?
Yes. Once downloaded, products are yours permanently — even if you cancel your subscription. Products downloaded under a Pro or Enterprise plan include the source files and extended commercial license. You can use them in unlimited client projects.
Is there a one-time purchase option?
Absolutely. You can purchase individual products from our marketplace without a subscription. Individual product prices range from $49 to $299. A subscription makes sense if you plan to download 3+ products, as it's more cost-effective.
Do you offer non-profit or student discounts?
Yes! We offer 50% off for verified students and 30% off for registered non-profits. Contact us at hello@nexusplatform.io with proof of eligibility to receive your discount code. Open source maintainers also qualify for our free developer plan.
What's included in Enterprise support?
Enterprise includes a dedicated account manager who knows your team and tech stack, a 1-hour response SLA (24/7 for critical issues), private Slack channel access, quarterly business reviews, custom onboarding sessions, and priority feature requests.
