Our Culture

A Team That Ships,
Learns, and Grows Together

We're building a company where talented people can do the most meaningful work of their careers. That means high autonomy, high trust, and a bias for doing things properly — not just quickly.

We review everything — code, design, copy, processes. We debate ideas openly and disagree respectfully. And we celebrate wins together, whether it's shipping a major feature or closing a key partnership.

We default to written communication. Meetings are optional, focused, and always have notes.

Deep Work Culture
Protected focus blocks. No Slack after 6pm. We optimize for deep work over constant availability.
