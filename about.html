Our Values

The Principles That
Guide Everything We Do

Craft Over Output

We measure success by quality, not quantity. Every component, every line of code, every pixel is considered and intentional. We'd rather ship one perfect thing than ten mediocre ones.

Accessible by Default

Great tools shouldn't only be available to well-funded teams. We price fairly, document thoroughly, and make sure our products work for developers at every stage of their journey.

Bias Toward Action

We move fast and learn from real usage. We ship, gather feedback, and iterate. A shipped product with a small flaw beats a perfect product that never launches.

Customer Obsession

Every feature, every design decision, every support response starts with one question: does this make our customers more successful? That question shapes everything.

Long-term Thinking

We build for the long run. Lifetime licenses, backward compatibility, and sustainable pricing mean our customers can rely on us as a partner — not just a vendor.

Always Learning

Web standards evolve. We invest in keeping every product current, and we encourage every team member to spend 20% of their time exploring new technologies and ideas.

Our Journey

From Side Project to
Platform of Choice

Q2 2025

The Beginning

Sagnik and Null built the first NexTSpace template as a side project. It sold 40 copies in the first week.

Q1 2026

First 1,000 Customers

Launched the UI Component Library to overwhelming demand. Hit 1,000 paying customers in 6 weeks. Raised a $1.2M pre-seed round to hire the first 4 engineers.

Q3 2026

Services Launch

Expanded beyond products into professional services. Onboarded our first 20 enterprise clients for custom development and design system work.

Q2 2027

$5M Series A

Closed a $5M Series A led by Benchmark Capital. Used the capital to expand the team to 18 people and launch the SaaS subscription model.

Q4 2029

Global Expansion

Reached 8,000 customers across 90 countries. Launched multi-language support and regional pricing. Named a Top 50 Dev Tools startup by Product Hunt.

Today — 2031

10,000+ and Growing

Over 10,000 customers, 200+ products, a team of 28, and a clear mission: to be the most trusted platform for digital products and professional services worldwide.
